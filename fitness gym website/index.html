Body Mass Index

Body mass index (BMI) is a medical screening tool that measures the ratio of your height to your weight to estimate the amount of body fat you have. Diagnosing weight types with BMI.

  • below 18.5 :
    Underweight

  • Between 18.5 to 24.9 :
    Healthy Weight

  • Between 25 to 29.9 :
    Overweight

  • Above 30 :
    Obese
